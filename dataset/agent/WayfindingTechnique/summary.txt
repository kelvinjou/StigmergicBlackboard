## WayfindingTechnique: Core Definition

Based on the ontology, **`:WayfindingTechnique`** is defined as:

> *"Methods that assist the user in maintaining and recovering spatial orientation during navigation."*

It is a subclass of:
- **`:InteractionTechnique`** → **`:Concept`**

This places wayfinding techniques within the broader family of interaction methods, distinct from tasks themselves (like `:WayfindingTask`) but designed to support them.
